Download video from Wistia

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7eFTEq5KBvg

Steps:

  1. Copy link and thumbnail from video

    (e.g.

    10x University - 10x Genomics)

  2. Get the one between “?wvideo=” and “#video” (e.g. hzbgpoju8a)

  3. Go to https://video-download.co/ and paste the link “fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/xxx” where “xxx” is the text you get from previous step (e.g. fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/hzbgpoju8a)

  4. Download the one you want
